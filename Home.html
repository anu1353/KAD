KAD Dancehouse

KAD Dancehouse is a centre for artistic endeavour. Founded in 2022 by the three young women KEERTHI,ANANDI AND DEEPA.
The Institution stands testimony to their dream of creating a space where the essence of Indian thought would find expression through artistic education.
An institution they established with the vision “of imparting to the young the true spirit of Art, devoid of vulgarity and commercialism.” It is a vital centre for study and performance of fine arts.
